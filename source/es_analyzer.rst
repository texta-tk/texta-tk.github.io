ES Analyzer
######################

:ref:`ES Analyzer <es_analyzer_concept>` is a tool for applying various :ref:`Elasticsearch <elasticsearch>` analyzers to :ref:`indices <index_concept>`. Currently supported analyzers are:

- Stemmer
- Tokenizer
